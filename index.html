Read Before Playing

Tournament Rules

Know the rules, respect the game. Violations result in immediate disqualification.

🎮 Game Rules

  • All 4 players must use their registered Free Fire UIDs
  • No hacks, mods, emulators, or third-party tools
  • Squad must be present in lobby 15 min before match
  • No intentional disconnection or leaving match
  • Room ID & password sent 30 min before on WhatsApp
  • 1 life per player — no respawns in custom room

💰 Payment Rules

  • Pay ₹50 per squad via UPI before submitting form
  • Enter valid UPI Transaction ID in registration
  • No refunds after registration is confirmed
  • Fake or invalid payment = disqualification
  • Prizes paid via UPI within 24 hours of results
  • Provide correct captain UPI ID for prize receipt

⚠️ Conduct Rules

  • No teaming with enemy squads (stream sniping)
  • No abusive language in lobby or group chat
  • Host's decision is final in all disputes
  • Report cheaters with video proof to host
  • One team registration per squad per tournament
  • Impersonating another player = permanent ban

📱 Technical Rules

  • All 4 squad members must be online before start
  • Stable internet is the player's responsibility
  • Join the WhatsApp community group for updates
  • Screenshot kills — required for Top Fragger claim
  • Match may be recorded for dispute resolution
  • Lobby must be joined on time — no waiting
